About us

About Hope Foundation

Hope Foundation is a platform which works for the welfare of poor people. Our goal is to provide basic necessities and essentials to those in need by connecting them with sponsors and NGOs.

22% of India's population — around 270 million people — live in poverty without access to proper food, water, or shelter. Hope Foundation makes charitable giving accessible, transparent, and instant.

You can view the details of any NGO, then accept or reject their fund request as a sponsor. As an NGO, you get the right to raise fund requests to any sponsor on the platform.
